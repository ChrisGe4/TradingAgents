recommendation, but **reject the immediate entry at $565**. We will execute the
long thesis only at the structural support level identified by the technical
analysis.

the **Risky Analyst**: *"Fraudulent companies... do not aggressively shrink
their float by returning
nearly $2 billion to the public markets."* This behavior is fundamentally incompatible with the "exit liquidity" or "laundromat" narratives proposed by the bears. The $

unverified "headline toxicity" of the CapitalWatch report.

evidence that the immediate technical environment is hostile. The **Safe Analyst

the stock is a "falling safe." Buying
at $565—when the 200-day Simple Moving Average (SMA) is sitting at $484—is an
unnecessary assumption of risk. As the **Neutral Analyst** stated, *"Buying at $
565 when the momentum is this bearish is premature... We trade the reality of
the price action, not the hope of the narrative."*

Therefore, we are buying the company (Fundamental Thesis), but we are refusing
to pay the current volatility premium (Technical Execution). We will not catch
the "falling knife" in mid-air; we will place our bucket on the floor.
