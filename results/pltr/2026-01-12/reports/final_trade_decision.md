### Lessons Learned

I’m recommending this because I’ve been burned by this exact setup before.

**Lesson from the 2021 SaaS Bubble:**

Twilio or Zoom) all the way down from their highs. I listened solely to the "
Risky" analysts who screamed about "Paradigm Shifts" and "Supercycles" while
ignoring that the stocks were trading at 50x Sales (not earnings, *sales*).

I learned that **a great company can be a terrible investment if bought at the
wrong price.**

Conversely, I have also panic-sold winners like Nvidia during 20% corrections,
only to watch them 10x later.

The lesson is **Balance**. The "Tactical Trim" protects us from the 2021
mistake (riding a bubble down), while the "Hold" protects us from the
panic-selling mistake (missing the long-term compounder). We take some chips off
the table, keep our seat, and wait for the cards to improve.
